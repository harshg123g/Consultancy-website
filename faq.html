What is the minimum number of employees required for PF registration?

PF registration is mandatory for establishments with 20 or more employees. However, voluntary registration is possible for establishments with fewer than 20 employees.

What is the contribution rate for PF?

The current contribution rate is 12% of the basic salary plus dearness allowance from both the employer and employee. However, there are certain exemptions and special cases that may apply.

What is the ESI contribution rate?

The ESI contribution rate is 4% of wages for employees and 3.25% for employers. This rate is subject to change based on government notifications.

How often should PF returns be filed?

PF returns must be filed monthly, along with the contribution payments. The due date is typically the 15th of the following month.

What are the benefits of ESI registration?

ESI provides comprehensive medical care to employees and their dependents, cash benefits during sickness and maternity, and compensation for employment injury.
